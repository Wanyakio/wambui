Our Programs

  • Boarding Facility: Provided to all children to enhance proper care, love and protection.
  • Physiotherapy/ Occupational Therapy Sessions: Provided for children to improve the body structure and mobility training.
  • Education: To ensure children learn good grooming skills, classroom learning and life skills.
  • Feeding Program: Offered to children with difficulties in feeding
  • Guidance and counseling services: Provided to parents and older children in order to attempt to improve relations and reunite families.

Mission

ORHSC aims at making a difference in lives of children in the society and hence meet their special demands today and be independent tomorrow.To achieve knowledge and practical potential for self-independence among children living with disabilities in our society.

Our Work

To provide quality care to children with special needs, orphans and vulnerable children. To give a sense of self-worth and esteem for children with special needs. To train and educate parents on handling children with special needs. To create awareness and importance of individuals with disabilities in the society.
